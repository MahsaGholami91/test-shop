Jardin d'Azur
Francis Collection
150 * 240 cm
  • overview

    French antique rugs like Aubusson or Savonnerie are well-known for their floral leafy details with eye-catching coloring what remind us French formal gardens that are designed based on symmetry and architectural aesthetic formulas. Jardin d'Azur is French equivalent of the phrase "Azure Garden" that is a perfect expression for this exquisite modern Persian rug by Boustani. Jardin d'Azur is symbol of classic French gardens in the interiors which donates liveliness, glory to the living spaces. Blue tonality of Jardin d'Azur is extracted from indigo as a natural dye that vitalizes handspun wool and natural silk. Jardin d'Azur | Francis collection is a lifelong tie between classic luxury and contemporary minimalism. When French art, history, culture is combined with Persian craftsmanship of handmade rug designing, weaving, a masterpiece can be expected. Modern Persian rugs of Francis collection by Boustani Glorious Handicrafts are other confirmations that beautiful culture and art can connect nations together across the world gloriously.

  • care

    When storing a rug (especially a heavy winter one), have it professionally cleaned and wrapped before storing in a climate-controlled area to avoid damage. If exposed to direct sunlight, rug colors typically fade over time, even if they are fade resistant. Clean spills immediately. Once a stain is set, it becomes much more difficult to clean. When pet hair accumulates in a rug, brush the rug vigorously with a stiff clothes brush or utility brush.
